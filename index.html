About Me

Frontend Devloper

A frontend developer is like the artist of the internet. They are the creative folks who design and build the parts of websites or apps that you see and interact with on your computer or phone. Think of them as the people who make a website look pretty and easy to use. They use languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create buttons, forms, images, and everything you click on or see on a website. They also make sure that a website works well on different devices, like your computer, tablet, or phone, so you can access it smoothly. So, if you ever visit a website that's beautiful and user-friendly, you can thank the frontend developer for their artistic and technical skills.
